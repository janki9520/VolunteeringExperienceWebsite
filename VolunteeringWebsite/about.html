KOSHISH: A Social Initiative

Social initiative by ISTE-ITNU in I-Fest'14.

Happiness is not measured by what we have but by what we give. Based on this premise, ISTE is organizing an event in which the youth, the future of the nation; will take a step forward to bring a change and make India a better place to live in.

The event will consist of a set of 16 tasks which are to be completed in a duration of 1 month with the fulfillment of the following objectives:

  • I- help
  • I- preserve
  • I- educate

Following are the tasks to be performed by the participants in order to contritbute to the society to bring a positive change:

  1. Organize a self-defense workshop for girls and women of any underprivileged area.
  2. Organize a tree plantation campaign in your locality.
  3. Organize talent hunt for blind/handicap children.
  4. Visit an underprivileged area and know about the hygiene problems and give appropriate solutions to people and implement few of those solutions.
  5. Utilize waste to create something useful or creative.
  6. Organize a Career Orientation Program for 10th /12th grade students of a municipal school. You may involve some authority or educational institutes.
  7. Organize a cloth donation campaign.
  8. Contribute to the nation: Undertake a task to disengage child labor from some place.
  9. Importance of hand sanitizing in govt. school and distribute the sanitizers.
  10. Cleanliness drive, installation of dustbin and hand gloves distribution
  11. Street play and rally on women empowerment-Arrange or organise anything you think would give happiness to someone. Marking will be done on the basis of your innovission (innovative vission)
  12. Sport event in underprivileged area
  13. Arrange a visit for orphanage children to old age home.
  14. Install composite toilets with NGO
  15. Arrange or organise anything you think would give happiness to someone
  16. Conduct a small scale workshop to teach a small scientific/creative model in a municipal school.

Each task will be assigned specific points and the participants will have to prove their contribution via reports, pics and videos as per the demand or criteria mentioned in the task.

Thus, teams from various colleges will participate and the top 10 teams will be called in Nirma during IFEST in which they will have to give their final presentation and they will be judged on the basis of the total points from the task+the presentation which will show their contribution.

Thus, KOSHISH will help the participants and the other people to realize the importance of taking a step towards making India a better nation altogether.
